Objective:

To understand how prompt quality affects AI responses by comparing weak and strong prompts for different tasks.

1. Resume Generation
Weak Prompt:
“Write a resume”
Strong Prompt:
“Write a professional resume for a final-year engineering student with skills in Python, SQL, and Machine Learning. Include projects, skills, and internship experience in a structured format.”
2. Business Idea
Weak Prompt:
“Give a business idea”
Strong Prompt:
“Suggest a startup business idea in the field of AI for road safety, including problem statement, solution, and target users.”
3. Study Plan
Weak Prompt:
“Make a study plan”
Strong Prompt:
“Create a 2-week study plan for learning Machine Learning basics, including daily topics, practice tasks, and revision schedule.”
Comparison:
Weak prompts are vague and lack details, so the output is generic.
Strong prompts provide clear instructions, context, and expected format, resulting in more accurate and useful responses.
Conclusion:

Prompt engineering plays a crucial role in improving AI output. Well-structured prompts with clear context and requirements produce better and more relevant results compared to vague prompts.
