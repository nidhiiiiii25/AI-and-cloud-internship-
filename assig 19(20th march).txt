2. Types of Challenges Identified
Slang / Abbreviations: omg, luv, idk, plz
Emojis: 😂, 😴, 🔥
Typos: beleive, hapy, amazng
Repeated Letters: sooo, everrrrr
Informal Language: gud, awsm
3. Preprocessing Techniques Needed
1. Lowercasing

Convert all text to lowercase
👉 Example: “OMG” → “omg”

2. Removing Emojis

Remove or convert emojis to meaning
👉 😂 → “happy”

3. Slang Normalization

Convert slang to proper words
👉 “luv” → “love”

4. Spelling Correction

Fix typos
👉 “beleive” → “believe”

5. Removing Repeated Characters

👉 “soooo” → “so”

6. Tokenization

Split sentence into words

7. Stopword Removal

Remove words like “is”, “the”

4. Conclusion

Messy text data contains slang, emojis, and errors, which make it difficult for machines to understand. Preprocessing helps clean and standardize the text for better analysis and model performance.
